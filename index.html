Last updated: July 2, 2026

Budgy does not collect your data. Everything you enter — accounts, balances, goals, bills, and debts — is saved only on your device. We don't operate servers that store your financial information, and we don't send it anywhere.

All information you enter into Budgy is stored locally in the app's storage on your iPhone. If you back up your device through iCloud or iTunes, your Budgy data may be included in that backup, governed by Apple's own privacy terms rather than ours.

Uninstalling Budgy removes its locally stored data from your device (unless preserved in a device backup you created). Since we never held a copy, we can't restore it for you.

Frequently asked questions

Since my data isn't in the cloud, how do I back it up?
Turn on iCloud Backup for your iPhone in Settings, and your Budgy data will be included the next time your device backs up — the same way your photos and messages are.
Can I use Budgy in a currency other than USD?
Yes. Budgy works with any currency — set your preferred currency in the app's settings and every total, goal, and bill will display in it.
What's the difference between Avalanche and Snowball?
Avalanche pays off your highest-interest debt first, saving the most money over time. Snowball pays off your smallest balance first, giving you faster wins to stay motivated. Budgy supports either strategy.
I deleted the app and lost my data. Can you recover it?
Because Budgy never stores your data anywhere but your device, we don't have a copy to restore. If you had iCloud Backup turned on, restoring your device from a recent backup should bring it back.
